# University of Technology FAQ

## General Information
- The University of Technology (U-Tech) is a private autonomous engineering and management institute established in 2005.
- Accredited with NAAC A+ grade and NBA accreditation for core engineering programs.
- Approved by AICTE and recognized under Section 2(f) and 12(B) of UGC Act.
- Affiliated with State Technical University for degree validation.
- Ranked in top 100 engineering colleges in India (NIRF category).
- Campus spread over 120 acres with academic blocks, hostels, research centers, and recreational areas.
- Located in Tech Valley, Sector 12, New City, easily accessible via metro and highways.
- Campus timings: 6:00 AM to 11:00 PM (academic access), hostels operate 24/7.
- Administrative office timings: 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM (Monday–Friday).
- Official Website: www.utech.edu
- Email: info@utech.edu
- Phone: +91-9876543210

- Admission channels:

- Application process:

  - Upload documents
  - Pay application fee
- Documents required:

  - Class 12 marksheet
  - Entrance exam scorecard

- Admission timeline:

  - Session starts: August

## Academic Structure
- Two semesters per academic year:
  - Odd Semester (Aug–Dec)
  - Even Semester (Jan–May)
- Each semester includes:
  - Mid-Semester Exams (30%)
  - End-Semester Exams (50%)
  - Assignments/Projects (20%)
- Credit system:
  - 1 credit = 1 hour lecture per week
- Typical load:
  - 18–24 credits per semester
- Attendance:
  - Minimum 75% mandatory
- Grading:
  - CGPA scale (0–10)
  - Grade mapping:
    - 9–10 → O (Outstanding)
    - 8–9 → A
    - 7–8 → B
    - Below 5 → Fail
